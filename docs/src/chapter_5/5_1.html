### 5.1: Moving Particles > The “visible” energies associated with an object would include its kinetic energy store if it is moving, and its gravitational potential energy store if it is lifted above ground level. > **But there is also a deeper, macroscopically-invisible store of energy associated with the particles of which the object is composed.**
### 5.1: Moving Particles * **Kinetic theory of matter:** matter is made of tiny particles constantly in motion * Particles have kinetic energy because they are moving * Particles also have potential energy because they oppose the bonds pulling them apart * The hotter a material is, the faster the particles move, the more its internal energy
