### 2.7 : Force, mass, acceleration ##### Newton's first law of motion > **Statement:** An object stays at rest, or continues to move in a straight line at constant speed, unless acted on by a resultant force. All matter has a built-in opposition to any change in its velocity. This property of matter is called **inertia**.
